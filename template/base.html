Important Notice to all guests


The Royal Malaysian Custom Department ("RMCD") is imposing a tourism tax (TTx) of RM10 per room per night for stays at accommodation premises in Malaysia. The tax will apply from and on 1 September 2017. This tax will apply no matter if the booking was made before or after the announcement, as stipulated in the Tourism Tax Act 2017

As a registered hotel operator, InnB Park Hotel will be required by law to collect this levy amount from all tourists living on the premise, under the TTx registration number 141-2017-10000713. For more information on the tourism tax, please refer to the Tourism Tax Act 2017 or visit the RMCD's website on www.myttx.customs.gov.my
